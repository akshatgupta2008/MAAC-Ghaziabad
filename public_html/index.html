VFX / Visual Effects Course

Master high-end digital compositing, rotoscopy, advanced particle FX, and 3D match moving using industry-standard tools like Nuke, Adobe After Effects, and Houdini. Create cinematic, studio-grade shots and build the exact technical skills needed to enter the booming global VFX industry.

Duration 18-24 Months Level Beginner → Pro

What you'll learn

  • Node-Based Digital Compositing: Master advanced multi-channel workflows, CGI pass integration, and deep compositing inside Foundry Nuke.
  • Rotoscopy, Paint & Wire Clean-up: Learn professional plate preparation, tracking-assisted rotoscoping, and digital artifact removal using Mocha.
  • 3D Match Moving & Camera Tracking: Understand perspective calibration, focal length matching, and seamless 3D element integration into live-action footage.
  • Green-Screen Keying & Elements Integration: Master core extraction techniques, spill suppression, edge blending, and photorealistic environmental matching.
  • Procedural Particle Dynamics & Real-Time FX: Step into simulation mechanics by creating realistic smoke, fire, fluid physics, and dynamic destruction.
  • Digital Matte Painting & Set Extension: Combine 2D digital artwork with 3D camera projections to build expansive, cinematic environments.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about joining our creative programs, from eligibility to placement support.

1. What is the eligibility criteria to join the career courses at MAAC Ghaziabad?

Anyone who has completed their 10th or 12th standard (from any stream) is eligible to join the programs at MAAC Ghaziabad. We teach everything from scratch, so beginners with no prior design or technical background are completely welcome.

2. Do I need to buy a high-end computer or laptop to practice at MAAC Ghaziabad?

Not at all! MAAC Ghaziabad is equipped with state-of-the-art RTX workstations, high-speed render farms, and Wacom tablets. You get full access to practice in our labs during center hours.

3. Will I get placement assistance after completing my course at MAAC Ghaziabad?

Yes, MAAC Ghaziabad provides 100% placement assistance. We have a dedicated placement cell and over 50+ active hiring partners across the Animation, VFX, and Gaming industries. We also conduct mock interviews and showreel reviews to make you job-ready.

4. Are the certificates provided by MAAC Ghaziabad recognized by employers?

Absolutely. All career courses at MAAC Ghaziabad provide government-recognized credentials that are formally aligned with the National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC) and the Media & Entertainment Skills Council (MESC).

5. How can I book a free demo class at MAAC Ghaziabad?

It's simple! You can fill out the enquiry form on this website, tap the WhatsApp button in the bottom corner, or directly call MAAC Ghaziabad. Our career counselors will schedule a free, guided demo session for you.
